### 4. **Multiple Readability Formulas** üìä
**Competitive with [Readable](https://readable.com/)**

Five industry-standard readability metrics:

1. **Flesch Reading Ease** (0-100 scale)
   - Higher = easier to read
   - Includes grade level interpretation

2. **Gunning Fog Index**
   - Estimates years of education needed
   - Based on complex word usage

3. **SMOG Index** (Simple Measure of Gobbledygook)
   - Accurate for technical/scientific writing
   - Based on polysyllabic words

4. **Coleman-Liau Index**
   - Character-based formula
   - More accurate for shorter texts

5. **Automated Readability Index (ARI)**
   - Used in educational settings
   - Character/word ratio based

**Why it matters:**
- Different industries prefer different metrics
- Comprehensive analysis builds credibility
- Target specific audiences (academic, marketing, technical)
